Abstract-We will be having patient and doctor (identification for verification purposes) who will be using this web app Patient will get an digital prescription from the doctor via email and also will be shown on the myprescription tab on the patient's web page.

The app will also provide info about the drug's side-effects, dosage, composition, reviews and will have a Pill Reminder to remind the patient to take his pill as per the time set by him(useful in case of old people to never miss their dose).

Nearby medical stores will be suggested to the patients where they can show the digital prescription and get the medicine. (the linkage with google map may provide the clue to all the Medical Shops and Drugs Stores present in the vicinity/ or the city selling the drug).
